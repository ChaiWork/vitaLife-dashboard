# VitaLife Assistant 🩺

VitaLife Assistant is an advanced, AI-driven health companion designed for comprehensive vital sign monitoring, chronic disease assessment, and clinical intelligence. Built with a focus on precision and proactive care, it bridges the gap between daily vitals logging and professional health analysis.

---

## 🌟 What is VitaLife?

VitaLife is more than just a health tracker; it's a **Personal Clinical Intelligence Dashboard**. It empowers users to take control of their health through high-fidelity data visualization and cutting-edge GenAI analytics.

### Core Capabilities:
- **📊 Professional Vitals Dashboard:** Real-time visibility into Heart Rate (BPM), Blood Pressure (mmHg), Glucose (mg/dL), SpO2 (%), and BMI.
- **🧠 Chronic AI Intelligence:** Specialized GenAI flows that analyze metabolic and hypertension trends to provide clinical-grade summaries and risk assessments.
- **📈 Trend Analytics:** Advanced charting that identifies stability scores, historical trends, and projects potential health outcomes for the next 7-30 days.
- **🤝 Caregiver Network:** A secure "Family Link" system allowing caregivers and family members to monitor vitals and receive emergency alerts.
- **🛡️ Clinical History Audit:** A deep audit trail of every AI assessment, providing a long-term view of your health trajectory.

## 🚀 Getting Started (Tutorial)

Follow these steps to maximize your health insights with VitaLife:

### 1. Secure Onboarding
- **Sign In:** Use your Google account for secure, encrypted access.
- **Profile Setup:** Complete your profiles (Age, Weight, Height) to enable accurate BMI calculation and personalized risk assessments.

### 2. Logging Your First Vitals
- Navigate to the **Dashboard**.
- Use the **Manual Log** entry form to input your latest Blood Pressure, Glucose, and Heart Rate readings.
- *Tip: Consistency is key! Log at the same time each day for the most accurate trend analysis.*

### 3. Activating AI Analysis
- Once your data is logged, look for the **Chronic Clinical Intelligence** section.
- Click **"Refresh Analysis"** to trigger a deep clinical scan.
- The AI will provide:
    - **Clinical Summary:** A professional assessment of your current state.
    - **Future Risks:** Early warnings of potential conditions.
    - **Preventative Directives:** Actionable steps to improve your vitals.

### 4. Exploring Trends & Predictions
- Use the **Trend Intelligence** tab to toggle between Daily, Weekly, and Monthly views.
- Review your **Stability Score**—a high score indicates consistent, healthy management.
- Check the **7-Day Prediction** to see where your health is trending based on current habits.

### 5. Building Your Care Circle
- Go to the **Caregiver** tab.
- Invite family members or your doctor by their email address.
- Invited members can view your dashboard in "Caregiver Mode," allowing them to assist in your health management.

### 6. Health Historical Audit
- Visit the **AI History** tab to see every analysis the AI has ever performed.
- Click on any entry to see the full clinical audit, including former medication suggestions and historical risk levels.

## 🏗️ Tech Stack & Architecture
VitaLife is built with a modern, high-performance stack:
- **Frontend:** React 18 with Vite, Tailwind CSS for glassmorphic UI.
- **Backend Infrastructure:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions).
- **AI Core:** [Google Genkit](https://js.langchain.com/docs/get_started/introduction) powered by **Gemini Pro/Flash**.
- **Data Visualization:** Recharts for ultra-smooth health trajectory mapping.
- **Security:** ABAC (Attribute-Based Access Control) using Firestore Security Rules.

---

## 🛡️ Security & Privacy
VitaLife utilizes **Enterprise-grade Firestore Security Rules** and **Zero-Trust Identity Management**. Your health data is strictly isolated and only accessible to you and your authorized caregivers.
